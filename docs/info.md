# Info

**viraled.lol** is an exclusive Content Creator Networking Platform built for established creators to grow, network, and streamline their publishing workflows.

## The First Stage: Gaining Access

We believe in quality and curation. Gaining access requires verifying your audience footprint:

1. **Sign Up**: Register using your email and password, or quickly authorize via Discord.
2. **Stat Display Stage**: You will have exactly 5 slots to authorize your main social profiles (Instagram accounts, YouTube channels, or TikTok accounts).
3. **Viraled Member**: If your combined following across those 5 slots reaches **150,000**, you unlock the "Viraled Member" rank and gain full platform access.

## Your Public Profile

Once you are a Viraled Member, a public creator profile is generated for others to see. In your site settings, you have full control over this profile—you can upload a custom profile picture and explicitly hide any specific statistics or content you prefer not to display.

## Core Features

Once inside, you navigate your success through three primary hubs:

### 1. Creator Dashboard
A highly efficient center for content distribution.
- Upload video files directly and schedule them to publish on YouTube, TikTok, and other platforms from one place.
- Batch select multiple videos (e.g. from TikTok) and push them over to YouTube seamlessly. 
- Confirm and meticulously manage the schedule, title, description, and hashtags for each piece of content before it goes live.

### 2. Connect and Network
Designed like a specialized Twitter or LinkedIn feed just for industry leaders.
- Discover high-performing content generated by top creators that is currently growing fast or has historically dominated.
- Use these insights exclusively for **content analyzing purposes** to see why top-tier videos succeed and how you can apply those lessons to your channel.
